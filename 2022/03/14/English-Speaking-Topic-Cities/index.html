English speaking topic: how to introduce a city.

When introducing a city, you may want to talk about…

  • Location / Size
  • History
  • Industry / Commerce
  • Interesting places / Culture
  • Weather

Example: Tianjin

Location / Size

  1. Tianjin is a big city. It can be found on the east coast of China.
  2. Tianjin is to the south of Beijing, about 100 kilometers.
  3. Tianjin’s located in the northeast of China in the Bohai Sea area.

History

  1. Tianjin has a history of more than 600 years since its establishment in 1404.
  2. In 1860, Tianjin was opened up as a trading port.

Industry / Commerce

  1. It relies mainly on trade and service industry.
  2. It’s famous for food and entertainment.
  3. Its main source of revenue comes from trade.
  4. The economy depends mainly on port trading and service industry.

Interesting places / Culture

  1. Places worth visiting include Haihe River and Huangyaguan Great Wall and Panshan mountain.
  2. It enjoys a reputation of a “cradle of operas” and “motherland of quyi”.
  3. If you could only do one thing in Tianjin, I would recommend tasting some local snacks.

Weather

  1. In Tianjin, the summers are long, hot and partly cloudy.
  2. The winters are freezing, snowy, and mostly clear.
